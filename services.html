AI Readiness Assessment

Not sure where your organization stands on AI? We start there. A focused engagement that maps your current tools, identifies your highest-value AI opportunities, and gives you a clear picture of what's realistic for your budget and team.

No assumptions, no generic frameworks. Everything is grounded in how your organization actually operates.

Deliverable: Written AI readiness report with prioritized recommendations
Timeline: 2 to 3 weeks

AI Strategy and Roadmap

For organizations ready to move from curiosity to action. We build a 12-month AI adoption roadmap tailored to your programs, staff capacity, and budget. Every recommendation is specific to how your organization works, not a template.

Deliverable: 12-month roadmap with phased implementation plan
Timeline: 4 to 6 weeks

Implementation Support

Strategy is only useful if it gets executed. We work alongside your team to deploy the right AI tools, train your staff, and make sure the technology sticks. Available as a fixed-scope project or an ongoing monthly engagement.
