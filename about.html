Technology Experience.
Nonprofit Roots.

Bill Wheeler, Founder — Wheeler Tech AI

I have spent more than 20 years building and securing enterprise technology systems for large organizations. I work at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, AI adoption, and the people who have to use these tools every day.

I have also spent years leading nonprofit organizations: as State President of the Illinois Society of the Sons of the American Revolution, as Director of PR and Technology for the Marine Corps Coordinating Council of Illinois, and as a former director of a regional genealogical society.

I know what it is like to sit across the table from a board, explain a technology decision on a limited budget, and make it work without jargon. That combination is what Wheeler Tech AI is built on. I am not an outsider trying to sell AI to mission-driven organizations. I am a peer who happens to have deep technical expertise.

What I Bring

  • 20+ years in cloud architecture and enterprise technology (Azure, GitHub, security)
  • Current AI adoption experience leading GitHub Copilot pilots in enterprise environments
  • GitHub Advanced Security certification
  • Active nonprofit leadership across multiple Illinois organizations
  • A track record of translating complex technology into plain-language decisions for boards and executive teams

Wheeler Tech AI is based in Illinois and works with organizations nationwide.
